Supper club

By Iestyn Gage

When: 23rd April Location:

Menu

Starter

Grilled Pear & Burrata Crostini

Charred slivers of pear atop toasted sourdough, layered with creamy burrata, a drizzle of honey-thyme vinaigrette, and crushed pink peppercorns. Garnished with micro basil and edible flowers.

Main

Peach-Glazed Duck Breast with Herbed Farro

Crisp-skinned duck breast glazed with a reduction of white peach, ginger, and rosemary. Served over warm farro tossed with spring greens, shaved fennel, and toasted hazelnuts.

Dessert

Pear & Peach Tarte Tatin with Chamomile Cream

Caramelized slices of pear and peach baked into a golden puff pastry base. Served warm with a dollop of whipped chamomile cream and a scattering of candied pistachios.
